About

Not a gamer. A builder.

I'm Cyber Slot - Krishnendu Maji, from Bankura, West Bengal. A years old developer.

My channel isn't a gaming channel; you won't find BGMI or Free Fire here. If you're curious, I occasionally play Minecraft and Roblox (Zz36339) - casually, not competitively.

The "P90" in my handle isn't a reference to the weapon either - YouTube generated @cyberslot-p90 for me automatically. I only learned what it meant afterward.

What actually holds my attention is computers, GDI-driven visuals, and math-based audio - Bytebeat formulas, custom waveform synthesis, and the kind of software that generates its own imagery and sound from a few lines of math.

Focus

What I spend my time on

Desktop tools in C#

WinForms and .NET apps - screen recorders, voice assistants, editors - with real attention to native feel and performance.

Reverse engineering

Pulling apart bytecode and binaries for the puzzle of it, then rebuilding what I find in a language I actually want to ship in.

GDI & Bytebeat

Procedural visuals and audio generated from formulas - no samples, no assets, just math rendered in real time.

Low-level systems

Hobby kernels and HALs in C and x86 Assembly - interrupts, disk I/O, and the bugs that only show up at ring zero.
